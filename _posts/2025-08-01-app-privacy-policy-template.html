Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ "now" | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Types of Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, crash reports, performance data
  • Location Information: Location data when required for functionality (with user permission)
  • User-Provided Information: Content and settings you actively provide

2.2 How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you
  • Automatically when you use the App
  • From third-party services (analytics tools)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve App services
  • Personalize user experience
  • Analyze App performance and usage
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Ensure App security
  • Comply with legal requirements

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except when:

  • We have your explicit consent
  • Required by law
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Working with trusted service providers (limited to service provision)

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Access controls and permission management
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff privacy training

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Account information: During account activity and reasonable period after deletion
  • Usage data: Typically no more than 2 years
  • Other periods as required by law

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: View personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Processing Restriction: Limit our processing of your information
  • Data Portability: Obtain your data in a common format

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.
  • Crash Reporting: Crashlytics, etc.
  • Cloud Storage: iCloud, Google Drive, etc.
  • Social Login: WeChat, Weibo, Apple ID, etc.

These services have their own privacy policies, which we recommend you review.

9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other means. Continued use of the App indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@yourapp.com
  • Address: Your company address
  • Phone: Customer service number

13. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China. Disputes shall be resolved through friendly consultation; if unsuccessful, submitted to the competent People's Court.
