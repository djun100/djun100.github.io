--- layout: legal title: "Mobile App Privacy Policy" description: "Privacy Policy Template for iOS and Android Applications" last_updated: "August 1, 2025" --- ## 1. Overview This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled securely. **Scope:** This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected through this App. ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Types of Information We Collect **Device Information** - Device model and manufacturer - Operating system version - App version number - Device unique identifiers **Usage Data** - App launch frequency and duration - Feature usage statistics - Crash reports and error logs - Performance metrics **Location Information** - Collected only when required for functionality - Requires explicit user authorization - Can be disabled in settings at any time **User-Provided Information** - User account information - User-generated content - Feedback and customer service communications ### 2.2 How We Collect Information - **Direct Collection:** Information actively provided by users - **Automatic Collection:** Data automatically generated during app usage - **Third-Party Services:** Through integrated third-party SDKs ## 3. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information for the following purposes: - **Service Provision:** Operating core app functionality - **User Experience:** Personalizing content recommendations and interface optimization - **Product Improvement:** Analyzing usage patterns, fixing issues, developing new features - **Security:** Detecting and preventing security threats - **Customer Support:** Handling user feedback and technical issues - **Legal Compliance:** Meeting legal and regulatory requirements ## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure **We do not sell your personal information.** We may share your information in the following circumstances: - **With your explicit consent** - **As required by law or government requests** - **To protect our legitimate interests, users, or public safety** - **With service providers** (limited to the scope necessary for service provision) ### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers We may share necessary information with the following types of third-party service providers: - Cloud storage service providers - Data analytics service providers - Customer service platform providers - Payment processing service providers All third-party service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. ## 5. Data Security We implement the following security measures to protect your information: **Technical Measures** - Data transmission encryption (TLS/SSL) - Data storage encryption - Access controls and authentication - Regular security vulnerability scanning **Administrative Measures** - Employee data protection training - Principle of minimum data access privileges - Regular security audits - Emergency response mechanisms **Physical Measures** - Servers hosted in security-compliant data centers - 24-hour monitoring and access control ## 6. Data Retention We retain your personal information only for necessary periods: - **Account Information:** During account existence and 30 days after deletion - **Usage Data:** Maximum retention of 24 months - **Log Data:** Maximum retention of 12 months - **Customer Service Records:** Maximum retention of 36 months Data exceeding retention periods will be securely deleted or anonymized. ## 7. Your Rights Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights: **Right of Access** - View personal information we hold about you - Understand how information is collected and used **Right of Correction** - Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information **Right of Deletion** - Request deletion of your personal information - Account deletion **Right to Restrict Processing** - Limit our processing of your personal information **Right to Data Portability** - Obtain your data in a common, machine-readable format **Right to Withdraw Consent** - Withdraw previously given consent (does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal)
### How to Exercise Your Rights To exercise the above rights, please contact us through: - **Email:** privacy@yourapp.com - **In-app feedback feature** - **Customer service hotline:** 400-XXX-XXXX We will respond to your request within 15 business days.
## 8. Third-Party Services This App integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies: **Analytics Services** - Google Analytics: [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) - Firebase Analytics: [Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics) **Crash Reporting** - Firebase Crashlytics: [Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/terms/crashlytics) **Social Login** - WeChat Open Platform: [Privacy Policy](https://weixin.qq.com/cgi-bin/readtemplate?lang=zh_CN&t=weixin_agreement&s=privacy) - Apple ID: [Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services. ## 9. Children's Privacy **We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.** If we discover that we have inadvertently collected children's personal information, we will: - Immediately stop collection - Delete collected information as soon as possible - Notify the child's parents or guardians If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. ## 10. Cross-Border Data Transfers Your personal information may be transferred outside of China for processing and storage. We will: - Ensure the receiving country/region has adequate data protection levels - Sign data processing agreements with recipients containing standard contractual clauses - Implement other necessary security measures ## 11. Policy Updates We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will: - Publish update notifications within the app - Notify you via email or push notifications for significant changes - Publish the latest version on our website **Continued use of this App indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.** If you do not agree with the updated policy, please stop using this App and delete your account. ## 12. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the People's Republic of China. Disputes arising from this Privacy Policy should first be resolved through friendly consultation; if unsuccessful, either party may file a lawsuit with a competent People's Court. ---
